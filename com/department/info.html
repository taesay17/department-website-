What is Computer Science?

Computers and the Internet are having a great impact on human life and society. We now live in the computer age — an era of information and connectivity. The Computer Engineering Department offers a unique opportunity for bright young minds to excel in computer science, software, and hardware engineering.

Students begin with introductory courses in programming and algorithm design that lay the foundation for key subjects such as data structures, operating systems, databases, and computer networks. Math and engineering courses help prepare for advanced projects and research.

In later years, students take specialized courses and a senior design project, working on real-life challenges. The department emphasizes a balance of theory and practice to build both understanding and hands-on experience.

Our department has six well-equipped laboratories for software and electronics. Students use them for assignments, projects, and research — developing both skill and creativity.
